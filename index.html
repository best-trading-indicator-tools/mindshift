Privacy Policy

At MindShift, your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We believe in complete transparency about how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Storage

We've designed MindShift with privacy in mind. Our app operates primarily on your device, meaning your personal information and usage data remain under your control. We do not maintain external servers for storing your personal data, and we do not track your usage patterns or behaviors.

Local Data Storage

All your app data, including your progress and preferences, is stored locally on your device. This approach ensures that you maintain complete control over your information and can delete it at any time by simply uninstalling the app or using the in-app reset function.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any of your information with third parties. While the app may require certain device permissions to function properly, these are used solely for providing the intended features and nothing more.

Your Privacy Rights

As a MindShift user, you have complete control over your data. This includes:

• The right to access all your stored information through the app's interface
• The ability to delete your data at any time using the app's reset function
• The option to opt-out of any future data collection features we might add

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your progress at any time by navigating to the Settings menu within the app and selecting the "Reset Progress" option. Please note that this action cannot be undone, so make sure you want to clear all your data before proceeding.

Q: Is my data secure?

Yes, your data security is our priority. Since all data is stored locally on your device and not on external servers, you maintain complete control over your information. The app uses your device's built-in security features to protect your data.

Q: How do I update the app?

MindShift updates are distributed through the App Store. To ensure you're using the latest version, enable automatic updates in your device settings or manually check for updates in the App Store.
